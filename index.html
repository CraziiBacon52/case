About

In the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic, one issue became very apparent. How were students going to go back to school? The conclusion that was reached was that we wouldn't go back--we would zoom in, presenting another problem. How were people who couldn't afford computers or didn't know how to use them going to be able to do this? The reason for this website is to help people learn how to use things like zoom, email, and google, as well as to provide a platform for people to see where to donate. I hope that this helps!
